Introduction

Questions to explore

  • which movies had the highest and lowest profit?
  • Which movies had the highest and lowest budget?
  • How many movies made a profit and how many made a loss?
  • Which year saw the highest profits?
  • Which year saw the lowest profits?
  • Is vote average correlated to profits?
  • Is there a correlation between budget and profit?
  • Do Genres, Directors or Cast members have an impact on profitability

    #lowest
    low= df[column].idxmin()
    low_calc=pd.DataFrame(df.loc[low])

    table = pd.concat([high_calc, low_calc], axis=1)

Conclusion¶

We have found that there is a weak positive correlation between votes and profit. Our exploration of the data shows that a high budget is not necessarily a determining factor for a high profit. However, we have founf that, overall, There is a relationship between budget profit. Therefore, we can conclude that if budget has an upward trend so will profit. We have to also take into consideration factors such as directors and cast members. These variable have an impact on profitibility. The top 5 cast member that appeared most in movies that made the most profit are: -Tom Cruise
-Tom Hanks
-Brad Pitt
-Robert De Niro
-Bruce Willis

The top 5 directors that made the most profitable movies are: Steven Spielberg 27 Clint Eastwood 21 Ridley Scott 16 Tim Burton 16 Robert Zemeckis 15

Other variable that may have an impact on profit, which were not shown in the data, are advertising. We've seen an increase in profitability over the years. This may be due technological advances such as the internet (web advertising), broadcast advertising such as TV adverts, and the evolution of the devices we use and have access to. These advances make it easier for the movies to be marketed and for a wider range of people to be exposed to them.

#Limitations¶

The sample size of the data was reduced significantly, from 10866 movies to 3855. This Compromises the integrity of the data as this sample size is too small to truely represent the population correctly. However, there where quite alot of missing values and rows that had values of zero, this also compromises the data as it is not a accurate representation of the data. Dropping the rows with values of zero in the Budget and revenue columns also affected the outcome of the analysis.
